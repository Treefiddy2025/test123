About Dogs on Lawn

Your trusted partner in pet waste management and pet wellness

Our Story

At Dogs on Lawn, we understand that a clean yard is essential for both pet and family health. What started as a simple waste removal service has grown into a comprehensive pet wellness company, dedicated to making pet ownership easier and more enjoyable for everyone.

We've expanded our services to include premium nutritional products and partnerships with local businesses like MikesBites.ca, ensuring your furry friends get the very best in both care and products.

Our Mission

We strive to create cleaner, healthier environments for pets and their families while promoting overall pet wellness through quality nutrition and enrichment products. Our commitment extends beyond waste removal to ensuring every aspect of your pet's life is happy and healthy.

Our Values

Environmental Responsibility

We use eco-friendly disposal methods and promote sustainable practices in all our services.

Pet Wellness

We believe in a holistic approach to pet health, from clean yards to proper nutrition.

Local Partnership

We support local businesses and artisans who share our commitment to quality pet products.
